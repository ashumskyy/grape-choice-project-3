Project Overview

We have all on occasion been tasked with the chore of selecting a bottle of wine for a house-warming gift or holiday party and found ourselves overwhelmed by the sheer volume of options on the shelf! So we set out to build a wine selection dashboard to make it easier for the occasional wine drinker to find a great wine.

The dashboard will display the Top 10 rated wines based on user inputs for price range and varietal preference. Additionally, the dashboard will generate a graph depicting the Top 50 wines ranked by price and a 'streaming' chart showing the number of wines by country.

We hope you enjoy the dashboard and your next grape choice!
